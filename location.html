Cartagena Colombia

This beautiful City better known as The Walled City and it is the pride of Colombia.

The sea breeze and the sunset that transform the colors of 400-year-old houses enchant visitors in Cartagena.

A world heritage Site, this city was founded by Pedro de Heredia in 1533.
